About Tiki-Taka Tiki-Talk

Soccer conversations built around the way the game moves.

We are an international soccer podcast built on fan interaction, live streaming, and the chaos of the sport we all obsess over. From GOAT debates to Champions League drama, and from unboxing collectibles to live watch along parties, we cover the biggest moments in football with energy and personality. Smart takes, good vibes, and fútbol culture all in one place.

Host

Gianni

Host Bio

Gianni

Gianni's first language is fútbol. An Argentine-Italian who has loved the game since he could crawl. He brings passion, experience, and ball knowledge to every episode. If he's not watching his team River Plate, you can find him cruising through the city or modding one of his cars. Once home, he puts on the chef hat and whips up delicious meals from his Italian-Argentine recipes.

Host

Vinny

Host Bio

Vinny

Vinny speaks soccer with the eye of a scout and the brain of an analyst. He has deep international football insight, with roots in Albanian culture and is a huge Juventus fan. Vinny turns every conversation into a full match report, bringing sharp debate, detailed perspective, and true football IQ to every episode. Away from the game, Vinny stays dialed in by obsessing over great movies, getting lost in guitar riffs, and building his health and performance wellness.
